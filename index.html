Hello there! Currently I'm working as an assistant in Academic Affairs Section of Sports Institute, Chengdu University of Technology. Last year I graduated from Foreign Languages & Cultrues Collge, CDUT, majoring in Translation.

My research interest now mainly lies at Computational Linguistics especially Machine Translation. I desire to create my own machine translation, and already named it Xiahua Translation. The goal of this is to do better translation than myself, and I'm pretty sure I will be surpassed.

My previous research is in Corpus Linguistics. I've done my graduation thesis of "A Corpus-based Study on Translator’s Style in the Translation of Animal Farm: A Case Study of Chinese Translations of Li Jihong and Rong Rude". It was selected as the Excellent Thesis and I was invited to present my thesis in the 6th Scientific Research Salon, FLC, CDUT.
